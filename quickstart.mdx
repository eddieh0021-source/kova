This guide walks you through everything you need to go from zero to a fully operational CAD system for your ER:LC community. By the end, you'll have a live dispatch board, a connected PRC API map, and a Discord audit log running — all in one session.

<Tip>
  Want to see Kova Hub before you commit? Check out the interactive demo at [kovahub.app/#demo](https://kovahub.app/#demo) to explore the dispatch board and MDT without creating an account.
</Tip>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with Discord">
    Head to [kovahub.app/login](https://kovahub.app/login) and click **Continue with Discord**. Kova Hub uses Discord OAuth exclusively — there's no separate account or password to create. After you authorise the connection in Discord, you'll be redirected straight to your dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your server">
    Once you land on the dashboard, click **Create Server**. Give your server a name that matches your ER:LC community — this is what your members will see when they join. Your new server is ready immediately; no approval or waiting period required.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite your community">
    From your server's overview page, copy the **server invite link** and share it with your members. Anyone who joins through the link will be able to log in with their own Discord account and access the CAD panel under the role permissions you configure.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect the PRC API">
    Go to **Manage Server → Integrations** and paste your PRC API key into the designated field. Kova Hub polls the PRC API every second, so once your key is saved, the live player map and real-time unit positions will appear on the dispatch board the next time an active session is running.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the Discord audit log">
    Still in **Manage Server → Integrations**, connect a Discord channel for event logging. Kova Hub will post an entry to that channel every time a record is created, updated, or deleted — giving you a complete, timestamped audit trail without any extra setup.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start dispatching">
    Open the **CAD panel** from your server dashboard and go live. Dispatchers can begin managing calls and tracking units immediately. Officers can open the Police MDT to run plate lookups, issue BOLOs, and file citations right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>
