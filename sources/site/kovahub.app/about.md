# Built for the community, 
by the community.

Kova Hub is a free, modern CAD system made specifically for ER:LC roleplay servers — no subscriptions, no paywalls, no compromises.

## What is Kova Hub?

Kova Hub is a full-featured Computer Aided Dispatch system designed from the ground up for ER:LC communities. It gives your server a professional operations platform without the cost, complexity, or limitations of generic CAD software.

From live dispatch boards and police MDTs to Discord audit logs and PRC API integration, everything your community needs is built in and ready to use the moment you sign up.

We started Kova Hub because the ER:LC roleplay scene deserved better tooling. Other platforms either cost money, come loaded with unnecessary features, or weren't designed with Roblox and ER:LC in mind. We built the platform we wished existed.

### Live & Real-Time

Every action — calls, units, dispatches — syncs instantly across all connected sessions. No refreshing required.

### Secure by Default

Discord OAuth login, per-server role permissions, and session isolation keep your community's data safe.

### Fully Free, Always

No premium tiers, no feature locks, no monthly fees. The full platform is available to every community at zero cost.
