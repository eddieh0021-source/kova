This Privacy Policy explains how Kova Hub collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Kova Hub CAD platform. By using Kova Hub, you agree to the practices described here. **Last updated: April 2, 2026.**

## 1. Introduction

Kova Hub ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy covers the information we collect when you access or use the Kova Hub CAD platform, how we use that information, and the choices you have with respect to your data.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

**Discord OAuth**
When you authenticate with Discord, we receive your Discord user ID, username and display name, avatar URL, and guild memberships.

**Roblox OAuth**
When you link your Roblox account, we receive your Roblox user ID, username and display name, and avatar thumbnail URL.

**Usage Data**
As you use the platform, we store server configurations, CAD session data, character and civilian records, and communication data such as radio transmissions and bodycam streams. Communication data is processed during active sessions only and is not permanently stored.

**Automatically Collected Data**
We automatically collect your IP address (used for rate limiting and security purposes), browser type, and session identifiers.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Authentication** — to verify your identity and maintain your session
- **Service operation** — to deliver all features of the CAD platform
- **Server management** — to enforce the settings and permissions configured by server owners
- **Security** — to detect and prevent abuse, unauthorized access, and malicious activity
- **Communication** — to respond to support requests and send service-related notices
- **Improvement** — to understand how the platform is used and to make it better

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted PostgreSQL) with in-memory caches used during active sessions.

- **Session data** expires after 7 days of inactivity.
- **Server configurations** are retained for as long as the server exists on the platform.
- **Temporary data** — including radio audio and bodycam streams — is held in memory only during active sessions and is never written to permanent storage.
- **Account data** is deleted when you log out or when a server owner removes their server.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Your data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

- **Within your server** — server owners and authorized members can access relevant CAD records as part of normal platform operation.
- **Third-party webhooks** — if a server owner configures external webhook integrations, data flows to those endpoints as configured.
- **Legal requirements** — we may disclose information when required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.
- **Safety** — we may share information where necessary to protect the safety of users or the public.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Kova Hub integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **Discord** — used for authentication and audit log webhooks
- **Roblox** — used for optional account linking and in-game data via PRC
- **Supabase** — used for hosted database storage

## 7. Cookies

Kova Hub uses a single session cookie: `connect.sid`. This cookie is HTTP-only, set to secure in production environments, and expires after 7 days. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies of any kind.

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including:

- **HTTPS** — all data in transit is encrypted
- **HTTP-only secure cookies** — session cookies are inaccessible to client-side scripts
- **Rate limiting** — to protect against brute-force and denial-of-service attacks
- **HMAC signature verification** — all incoming webhooks are verified to prevent spoofing
- **Server-side database keys** — database service credentials are never exposed to the client

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights with respect to your personal data, including the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Correct** — request that inaccurate data be corrected
- **Delete** — request deletion of your personal data
- **Object** — object to certain types of processing
- **Portability** — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the [Kova Discord](https://discord.gg/qpb2pnBHS4) or through the platform's contact methods.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Kova Hub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 11. International Users

Kova Hub is operated from infrastructure that may be located in jurisdictions other than your own. By using the platform, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and processed in different jurisdictions, which may have different data protection laws than those in your country of residence.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Kova Hub after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## 13. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us via the [Kova Discord](https://discord.gg/qpb2pnBHS4) or through the platform's other contact methods listed on the [Contact](/contact) page.
