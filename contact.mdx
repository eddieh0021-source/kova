The Kova Hub team is here to help you get the most out of your CAD deployment. Whether you've hit a technical snag, have a feature idea, or need help getting your server configured, you can reach us through email or our community Discord server — choose whichever works best for you.

<Note>
  Discord is the fastest way to get real-time help. The support team and community are active there daily, and you'll often get a response within minutes during peak hours.
</Note>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email Support" icon="envelope" href="/contact">
    Send a message to **support@kovahub.app** for any of the following:

    - **Bug Report** — something isn't working as expected
    - **Feature Request** — an idea you'd like to see added
    - **Account Issue** — login problems or account recovery
    - **Server Setup** — help configuring your Kova Hub server
    - **Billing / Access** — questions about your server access or permissions
    - **Other** — anything that doesn't fit the categories above

    Email is best for detailed issues where you need to attach screenshots or describe a complex problem in writing.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Discord Community" icon="discord" href="https://discord.gg/qpb2pnBHS4">
    Join the **official Kova Hub Discord** at [discord.gg/qpb2pnBHS4](https://discord.gg/qpb2pnBHS4) for:

    - **Real-time support** from the Kova Hub team
    - **Announcements and release notes** — be the first to know about updates
    - **Feature requests & community feedback** — shape the direction of the platform
    - **Community discussion** with other ER:LC server owners and dispatchers

    Discord is the recommended channel for quick questions and live troubleshooting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

When reaching out, it helps to include your Kova Hub server name, a clear description of the issue, and any error messages or screenshots you have — this lets the team get to the bottom of things without needing to go back and forth for basic details.
