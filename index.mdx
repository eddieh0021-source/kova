## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with Discord">
    Go to [kovahub.app/login](https://kovahub.app/login) and click **Continue with Discord**. Kova Hub uses Discord OAuth — no separate account needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or join a server">
    Create a new Kova Hub server for your community or ask your server owner for an invite link. Each server is fully isolated with its own data, roles, and settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your integrations">
    Paste your PRC API key in **Manage Server → Integrations** to enable the live map and 911 webhook. Optionally connect a Discord channel for the audit log.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Kova Hub is 100% free — no credit card required, no trial period, no locked features.
</Note>
