Kova Hub gives your ER:LC roleplay community a professional-grade Computer Aided Dispatch system — completely free. Whether you run a small patrol server or a large multi-department operation, Kova Hub brings live dispatch, a full police MDT, and deep Discord integration together in one place.

## What is Kova Hub?

Kova Hub is a web-based CAD platform purpose-built for [Emergency Response: Liberty County (ER:LC)](https://www.roblox.com/games/2534724415) servers on Roblox. It connects directly to your in-game session through the PRC API, giving dispatchers and officers a real-time view of what's happening on the map — no spreadsheets, no manual radio logs, no third-party subscriptions required.

You log in with Discord, set up your server in minutes, and your entire community can start roleplaying with a fully operational dispatch system the same day.

## Who is Kova Hub for?

Kova Hub is built for **ER:LC server owners and staff** who want to run structured, realistic emergency roleplay without paying for enterprise software. It's a great fit if you are:

- A **server owner** looking to add a professional CAD and MDT to your community.
- A **dispatcher** who needs live unit tracking and call management in one tab.
- A **patrol officer** who wants quick access to plate lookups, warrants, and citation tools.
- A **staff member** who needs a reliable audit trail of every in-CAD action.

No prior CAD experience is required. If your community can use Discord, they can use Kova Hub.
