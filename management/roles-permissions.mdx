Kova Hub uses role-based access control (RBAC) to determine what each member can see and do within your server. Roles are scoped per department, so a member who is a supervisor in your Police department can have entirely different permissions in Fire/EMS — or no access there at all. Getting your roles right from the start keeps your CAD panel clean and your data secure.

<Info>
  Discord OAuth handles identity verification — Kova Hub knows *who* a member
  is because they log in with Discord. Kova Hub roles then control *what* that
  member is allowed to do once they're inside your server.
</Info>

## Role levels

Kova Hub has three built-in authority levels that form the backbone of every server's permission structure.

| Level | Scope | What they can do |
|---|---|---|
| **Server Owner** | Entire server | Full access to all departments, settings, integrations, and member management |
| **Department Admin** | One department | Manage members, roles, and CAD activity within their assigned department |
| **Member** | One department | Access the CAD panel according to the custom role assigned to them |

You can refine what members and department admins can do further by creating custom roles inside each department.

## Creating custom roles

Custom roles let you model your real community hierarchy — for example, Cadet, Officer, Sergeant, and Lieutenant — each with a tailored permission set.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open department settings">
    Go to **Manage Server**, select the department you want to configure, then
    open the **Roles** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new role">
    Click **Add Role**, enter a name that reflects the rank or function (for
    example, "Patrol Officer" or "Dispatcher"), and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set permissions">
    Toggle on the specific permissions this role should have. Permissions are
    applied only within the department where the role lives.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning roles to members

Once your custom roles exist, you can assign them to any member in that department.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Members list">
    In **Manage Server**, select the relevant department and open the
    **Members** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the member">
    Find the member you want to update and click their name to open their
    profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a role">
    Use the **Role** dropdown to assign the appropriate custom role. The change
    takes effect immediately — no page refresh required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Discord role sync

If your community uses Discord roles to track ranks or clearances, you can mirror that structure directly into Kova Hub without manually re-assigning everyone.

When Discord role sync is enabled, Kova Hub maps a Discord role to a Kova Hub role within a department. Members who already hold the mapped Discord role receive the corresponding Kova Hub permissions automatically when they log in.

<Tip>
  Discord role sync works best when your Discord hierarchy already reflects
  your in-game rank structure. Set up your Discord roles first, then map them
  in Kova Hub to save time on bulk assignments.
</Tip>

## Per-department access

Each department operates as its own permission boundary. A member must be explicitly assigned to a department before they can access its CAD panel — there is no global membership. This means you can:

- Give your Police supervisors elevated permissions without affecting your Fire/EMS panel.
- Allow a member to serve in multiple departments simultaneously, each with a different role.
- Restrict a probationary member to read-only access in their department while they complete training.

Department admins can only manage permissions and members within the departments they administer; they cannot alter settings for other departments or server-wide configuration.
